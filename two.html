WHAT IS YEAR (PRONOUNCED /’yir/)

A year is the amount of time that it takes for the Earth to make a full revolution around the sun. Well, not exactly. It takes slightly longer than a year so we don’t count the extra quarter of a day. After four years a year has an extra day in February because of this.

Instead of saying that the year is, for example, “2026.06,” we use months and dates to specify. For example, “January 23rd, 2026.” This is to make it easier to tell the date, especially for kids in ƨchool.
