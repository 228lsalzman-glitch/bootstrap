When does the 26-27 school year take place?

Although a year takes place within a single year, a school year starts at the end of the year and goes into the beginning of the following year.

Although it says “26-27,” the school year actually takes place between the years 2026 and 2027. 26 and 27 are abbreviations of those years. If we were actually in the year 26, then Pontius Pilate would have just become the governor of Judaea. Obviously, this happened over 2,000 years ago.
